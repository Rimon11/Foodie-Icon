Introducing the Foodie Coin

A unique digital asset that powers consumer deals for all areas of the food world (festivals, food trucks, restaurants ect..)

What is Foodie Coin?

Foodie Coin is a platform built for the future of the culinary world. It will be running on top of the Ethereum blockchain. As fellow food lovers and avid investors, this system has been built to accelerate growth of food trucks, restaurants, and festivals by offering one of a kind tools and services that save both time and significantly help with growing their customer base. After speaking with leaders in the food industry all over the world, some of their top complaints stem from payment processors taking too long to pay them, endless wasted spending on fees, and always struggling to find new marketing vehicles to fill the seats in their restaurants. We listened, and have found the solution for this problem.
